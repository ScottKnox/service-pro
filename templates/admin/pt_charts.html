Common Refrigerant Reference

Use these cards as a fast field reference for the refrigerants your technicians are most likely to see. For exact pressure-to-temperature values, always confirm with the equipment manufacturer chart and compare against actual operating conditions.

  • Use dew point for superheat on blended refrigerants when applicable.
  • Use bubble point for subcooling on blended refrigerants when applicable.
  • Confirm refrigerant type before charging, recovering, or diagnosing.
