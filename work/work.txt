# Feature: Owner Defined Recommendations and Good Better Best

## Context
Klōvent is a Flask-based multi-tenant field service management
platform for HVAC companies. When a tech builds a job estimate
the app should suggest relevant additional services and allow
generating a good better best estimate automatically.

Recommendations are defined by the company owner — not
generated by automated rules. This keeps suggestions relevant
to each company's specific business and price book.

## What this feature needs to do:

### 1 — Price book upsell configuration
In the price book each service or part item can have a list
of suggested upsells attached to it. These are other items
from the same company's price book.

When the owner edits a price book item they see a section:
"When this is added to a job — also suggest these"

Owner can add or remove any other price book items as
suggestions for that service. No limit on how many.

Store upsell relationships on the price book item itself
as an array of referenced price book item IDs.

### 2 — Featured recommendations
A separate company level setting — independent of any
specific price book item — defines a list of items to
suggest on every job regardless of what services are
on it.

Owner configures this in their settings or price book page.

Store as an array of price book item IDs on the company
notification or settings record.

Typical use: maintenance agreements, UV lights,
smart thermostats — items the owner always wants techs
to consider offering.

### 3 — Recommendations panel on the estimate builder
When a tech adds a line item to an estimate or job
the recommendations panel updates to show:

Section 1 — Recommended for this job:
Items linked as upsells to any service currently
on the estimate. Deduplicated if multiple services
suggest the same item. Do not show items already
on the estimate.

Section 2 — Always available:
The company featured recommendations list.
Do not show items already on the estimate.

Each recommendation shows:
- Item name
- Price from price book
- Add button — adds directly to estimate line items
  using existing line item logic

### 4 — Good better best estimate generation
Add a generate good better best button to the
estimate builder.

When tapped it pre-populates a new estimate with
three options using the existing estimate structure:

Essential:
Line items already on the current estimate.
No additions.

Recommended:
Everything in Essential plus all service specific
upsell suggestions for items on the estimate.

Comprehensive:
Everything in Recommended plus all featured
recommendation items.

Deduplicate across all three tiers.
Do not add items already present in a lower tier
to a higher tier.

Tech reviews and adjusts all three options before
sending. Use the existing estimate builder and
sending flow. Do not create new sending logic.

### 5 — Display
Recommendations panel should be visible alongside
the estimate line items while the tech is building
the job. Not a separate page. Not a modal.
Inline and always visible while estimate is open.

On mobile the panel collapses to a count badge
showing how many recommendations are available.
Tap to expand.

## Acceptance criteria:
- Owner can add upsell suggestions to any price book item
- Owner can configure company wide featured recommendations
- Recommendations panel updates when line items change
- Items already on estimate do not appear as recommendations
- Good better best button generates three tier estimate
- Existing estimate builder and sending flow used throughout
- Each tier correctly contains the right items
- No duplicate items within or across tiers

## Edge cases:
- Price book item has no upsells defined — show featured
  recommendations only in section 1
- No featured recommendations configured — hide section 2
- All recommendations already on estimate — show empty
  state message — your estimate is already comprehensive
- Tech dismisses a recommendation — hide it for remainder
  of this estimate session, do not permanently remove it
- Price book item referenced as upsell is later deleted —
  remove it from upsell suggestions silently, do not error
- Good better best tapped with no recommendations available —
  generate single tier estimate with items already on job,
  notify tech no additional recommendations exist
